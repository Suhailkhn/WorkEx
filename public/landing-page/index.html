Benefits

Updating your Resume

Have you ever sat down to update your resume but can't think of stuff to write?
You might have done great things but if you can't remember it and don't have a list of all things you have done, you are selling yourself short.

Acing Interviews

You should have a clear picture about your accomplishments, challenges and failures at your current job to be able to talk about them in detail.
Often candidates cannot quantify their achievements or are fuzzy about how they did something or what they did and hence cannot prove their worth in the interview.

Career Trajectory

Looking at this log will give you a true insight into how you are growing in your current job and whether it is time to move on.
When it comes to the manager's word against yours, you can trust this log to tell if you deserve better than what you are given credit for.
